Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AI & Lotto collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website.

1. Data Collection

We do not store your personal photos on our servers. The AI Animal Face Test process images locally in your browser using TensorFlow.js. When you use the webcam or upload a photo, the image data is processed on your device and discarded immediately after classification.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our site or other sites on the Internet.
  • Formspree: Our partnership inquiry form is powered by Formspree. Data you submit via the form is sent to our email.
  • Disqus: We use Disqus for our community comments section. Disqus may collect information as described in their own privacy policy.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to save your theme preferences (Dark/Light mode). Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Page.

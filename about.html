About AI & Lotto

Welcome to AI & Lotto, your unique destination for exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and personal luck. Our mission is to provide fun, engaging, and innovative tools that bring a little extra excitement to your day.

The AI Animal Face Test

Our flagship feature uses advanced machine learning models (powered by TensorFlow.js and Teachable Machine) to analyze facial features and classify them into popular "animal face" types. Whether you're a Dog type or a Cat type, our AI provides a scientific yet playful perspective on your persona.

The Lucky Lotto Generator

We believe everyone needs a bit of luck. Our lotto generator uses cryptographically secure random number generation to provide you with a fresh set of lucky numbers for your next lottery attempt. It's simple, fair, and fun!

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to building high-quality, user-friendly applications that respect your privacy and provide genuine value. We are constantly improving our models and adding new features based on community feedback.
